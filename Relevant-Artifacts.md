## ITIL 4 Foundational Definitions

The following table provides the exact, verbatim definitions for the core concepts managed within the "High-Latency Six" categories, directly extracted from the official **ITIL 4 Foundation Glossary** (Axelos, 2019). These serve as the traditional baseline before the introduction of Agentic AI.

| Concept / Entity | Exact ITIL 4 Glossary Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Incident** | An unplanned interruption to a service or reduction in the quality of a service. |
| **Service Desk** | The point of communication between the service provider and all its users. |
| **Service Request** | A request from a user or a user’s authorized representative that initiates a service action which has been agreed as a normal part of service delivery. |
| **Change** | The addition, modification, or removal of anything that could have a direct or indirect effect on services. |
| **Problem** | A cause, or potential cause, of one or more incidents. |
| **Monitoring** | Repeated observation of a system, practice, process, service, or other entity to detect events and to ensure that the current status is known. *(Note: An **Event** is defined as "Any change of state that has significance for the management of a service or other configuration item.")* |
